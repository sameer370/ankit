Western India comprises of the states of Goa, Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and the union territory of Daman & Diu. It is primarly famous for places like Mumbai, Goa, Rann of Kutch, the cave temples of Ajanta & Ellora and the Wildlife Sanctuaries in Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh. Boasting of a heritage, this part of the country houses some exemplary tourist sites including magnificient monuments, which are embodiments of the glorious architectural heritage of ancient India. Besides , the temperate climate in west India supports the ggrowth of uniquely varied flora and fauna.

Goa presents an exhilarating combination of an unending expnse of spectacular beaches, sensuous golden sands, rich cultural heritage, varied flora and fauna, beautiful churches and magnificient temples. Madhya Pradesh is often called the heart of India with a landscape of plateaus, river valleys, hills and forests.

Gujarat. is known for its ancient temples, spectacular beaches, colorful people and the rich cultural heritage.The home state of Mahatma Gandhi, the Father of the Nation, Gujarat boasts of beautiful temples, sunny beaches, the Rann of Kuttch, several historic sites,national parks and sanctuaries.

The twin city of Daman & Diu is situated on the southern border of the state of Gujarat. They are beautiful gateways with golden beaches, dense plm groves and historical monuments.
