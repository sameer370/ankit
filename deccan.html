The Deccan Odyssey

The Train

The Deccan Odyssey recreates the Royal Past of the Marathas. Launched in January 2004, Deccan Odyssey is a collaboration of the Indian Railways and the Maharashtra Tourism. The week long journey transports it’s passengers to the touristic delights of Maharashtra. Starting from the pulsating city of Mumbai, this Maharaja train takes you to the grandeur and the majestic sea forts of Sindhudurg, the beautiful village of Tarkali, the fun and frolic at the Goa beaches, breathtaking wildlife safaris of Tadoba, and lastly winding up at the world famous caves Ajanta and Ellora.

From its plush interiors, the Cabins are luxuriously furnished with private attached Bathroom, Shower & Toiletries, 4 Channel Music Facility,Direct Dialing Facility, Electronic Safe & a Small Cupboard.The other facilities provided include one Bar Lounge Car, Two Dining Cars with Onboard Kitchen, Mini-gym with Spa Saloon; & Conference Saloon Car with Mini-business Centre.
