SOUTH INDIA
Location:Below the Vindhya Range
Major Cities: Chennai, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Cochin, Pondicherry.
Holy Destinations: Rameshwaram, Sabarimala, Thiruvananthapuram
Holiday Destinations: Alappuzha Backwaters, Southern Beaches, Lakhshadweep

South India is linguistic and geographically cultural region of India. South India captures the Vindhyanchal mountain ranges, Sutpura and Narmada River. South India is linguistic and geographic culture land of India.

Soth India basically covers the four exotic states of India: Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala and Tamil Nadu and the union Territory of Pondicherry. The south India tourist destinations are tourism hot spots offering a right blend of wildlife, beaches, temples, history, landscapes that allure millions of tourists every year.

The backwaters of Kerala, pristine beaches of Lakshadweep, temples of Tamil Nadu, the wildlife belt of the Mudumalai and Bandipur forests, the world heritage sites of Karnataka is what makes the visit to south India perfect vaction.
