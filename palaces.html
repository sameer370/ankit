Palace on Wheels
TCI brings you a very special offer for a very special experience. It is a remarkable journey through an extraordinary region of India on the Palace on Wheels. Times have changed and the winds have shifted, but the legendary Palace on Wheels crosses the states of Rajasthan in a week long journey. You will travel back in time in royal style, to a colourful world of maharajas and palaces, fortresses and legendary warriors.

The Palace on Wheels has 14 air conditioned coaches, named after the former Princely States and is a recreation of the personal railway coaches of the royals. Each coach consist of four cabins with double or twin beds, attached bathroom with hot and cold water showers, built in wardrobes, plush decor, piped music and personal attendants at your service. In addition, the fully carpeted train has a lounge area, a library and a bar and two dinning cars servicing Continental, Indian and Rajasthani cuisine.....in fact everything that would ensure an unforgettable experience for a king.

Itinerary
Days City Snap Detail
1.Wed Delhi Board the Palace on Wheels at 1700hrs at Delhi Cantonment Railway Station. Depart at 1830hrs at Jaipur. Dinner at board.
2.Thurs Jaipur Arrive at 0700hrs. Breakfast on Board. Welcome to the Pink City, Jaipur. Visit Hawa Mahal and Amber Fort, both silent sentinels of a glorious era. Lunch at a palace hotel.Visit Jantar Mantar, the age old Observatory and City Palace, home to the erstwhile rulers. Depart for Jaisalmer at 1930 hrs. Dinner on the board.
3.Fri Jaisalmer Arrive at 0815hrs. Breakfast on board. Morning sightseeing of this 12th century fort. Visit the exquisitely carved havelis and palaces with their latticed windows, balconies, cupolas and facades. Lunch on board. At 1700 hrs visit the sand duned and take a camel ride. Dinner with Cultural programme at a palace hotel. Depart at 2345hrs for Jodhpur.
4.Sat Jodhpur Arrive 0700 hrs. Breakfast on board. Visit the magnificent Mehrangarh and Jaswant Thada, the royal cenotaph. Lunch at palace hotel. Depart for Sawai Madhopur at 1530 hrs. Dinner on board.
5.Sun Sawai Madhopur-Chittaurgarh Arrive 0400 hrs. Breakfast on board. Depart by motorcoach for a Safari in Ranthambor National Park. This Project Tiger Reserve surrounds he imposing ramparts of the 10th century fort. Depart at 1030 hrs for Chittaurgarh. Lunch on board. Arrive at 1600 hrs. Visit the Chittaurgarh Fort, lauded in legends of valour and chivalry. Depart 2000 hrs for Udiapur. Dinner on the board.
6.Mon Udiapur Breakfast on Board. Proceed to Udaipur by motorcoach. Sightseeing of Udaipur, the city of Lakes, Palaces, Gardens, Temples and Forts. Lunch at the Lake Palace, the former summer residence of the Princes of Mewar. Enjoy the tranquillity of Lake Pichola. Visit the City Palace in all its ancient glory. Return to train at Chittaurgarh and depart for Agra at 1830 hrs. Dinner on board
7.Tues Bharatpur-Agra Arrive at 0600 hrs at the Kepladeo Ghana National Park at Bharatpur, which is home to 300 species of birds, some flying in from as far as Siberia and China. Breakfast on board. Depart at 1145 hrs for Fatehpur Sikri, the red sandstone palatial complex built by Emperor Akbar which now stands deserted. Lunch on board. Depart for Agra and arrive at 1430 hrs. Visit the Agra Fort and the beautiful Taj Mahal. Dinner on board. Depart Agra at 2300 hrs.
8.Wed Delhi Arrive at 0630 hrs at Delhi Cantonment Railway Station. Breakfast on board.
End of Tour
